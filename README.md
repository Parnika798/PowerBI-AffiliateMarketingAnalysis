## üéØ Project Objective

To solve the challenge of **fragmented affiliate data** by building a **unified, interactive Power BI dashboard**.
The goal was to provide clear, actionable insights into **sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends**, enabling data-driven decision-making for marketing strategists and executive leadership.

---

## üìà Key Achievements & Strategic Insights

This analysis translated complex data into clear business outcomes, identifying critical opportunities for growth and efficiency:

* **Identified Top Market:**
  Uncovered that **Canada** is the dominant revenue-generating market, outperforming the US and presenting a key opportunity to analyze and replicate its success.

* **Pinpointed High-Efficiency Channels:**
  Confirmed that **Social Media** is the top acquisition channel, driving the majority of user sessions and proving its value in the marketing mix.

* **Unlocked a Growth Strategy:**
  Revealed that the business model is heavily reliant on **new customer acquisition**, highlighting a strategic need to develop **retention programs** to increase customer lifetime value.

* **Delivered Actionable Customer Segments:**
  Used **advanced analytics** to segment users into three key personas ‚Äî
  **High-Value Champions, Core Customers, and Window Shoppers** ‚Äî providing a clear framework for targeted campaigns to convert low-engagement users.

## üîÑ Technical Workflow

This project followed a **structured, end-to-end analytical process** to ensure a robust and accurate final product.

1. **Data Cleaning & Transformation**

   * Ingested four raw CSV files.
   * Performed rigorous cleaning in Power Query: corrected data types, handled nulls, and shaped data for modeling.

2. **Data Modeling**

   * Architected an efficient **Star Schema** with clean dimension tables (`Dates`, `Sessions`, `Products`) and one-to-many relationships to fact tables (`Clicks`, `Conversions`).
   * Ensured **data integrity** and **optimal query performance**.

3. **DAX Calculations**

   * Created a centralized `_Key Measures` table containing over **15 complex DAX measures** such as `Total Revenue`, `Conversion Rate`, and `Total Commission`.
   * These measures powered all dynamic visuals across the report.
